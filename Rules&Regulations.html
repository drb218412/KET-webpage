Rules & Regulations

For you safety, we ask that you follow these simple rules when riding our trains and fire truck:

  • All passengers must wear their seatbelt
  • One adult or teenager per seat
  • 250lb max per seat
  • Patiently wait your turn
  • Keep you hands and feet inside the cars at ALL TIMES
  • Do not climb on the vehicle or its cars
  • Respect the cars and do not kick or stomp on the cars
  • Do not stand while the vehicle is in motion
  • Do not exit the vehicle until it is fully stopped
  • If you are not riding, do not hang onto the vehicle while it is in motion
  • Do not jump onto the vehicle while it is in motion
  • Always listen to your driver!

Thank you for your cooperation and have a safe ride!
