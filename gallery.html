We are ever expanding our attractions to accomodate our client's needs. Our fleet can run on any smooth service including concrete, asphalt, dirt, and gravel. Amongst our fleet are steamless trains, steam producing trains, a fire truck, a large tram train, a barrel train, and a bounce house. Meet our fleet below!

Meet Rufus!

Our first and original king, this train has been with us since the beginning. It has a steel engine that produces genuine smoke from its tall steel smoke stack. It pulls three colorful wooden cars that seat up to 18 people including adults and special needs persons. This train is fitted with a loud horn and bell to mimic genuine train sounds. This train is fueled with propane for an environmentally friendly ride you can feel good about. This train comes with a driver dawning an appropriate train conductor uniform.

Meet RayRay!

This train is retrofitted to produce genuine smoke from its smoke stack. It is complete with a custom made fiber-glass facade to mimic an actual train engine. It also has a horn and bell to mimic train sounds. This train is perfect for fulfilling any childhood fantasy. Its fiber glass cars are accessible to adults and children alike, including special needs persons. Each of our colorful cars are conveniently covered with a hood to match. There are a total of 6 seats which can fit up to 18 people. This train comes with a driver dawning an appropriate train conductor uniform.

Meet our Fire Truck!

Our firetruck is custom fitted with a red fiber-glass facade painted to mimic a red fire engine. It comes complete with decorative features to include a toy ladder and fire extinguisher. Its best feature is a bell and loud fire engine horn that mimics the sounds of an actual fire engine. One of our drivers dawns a genuine fireman's jacket and helmet for a full effect and is perfect for any childhood fantasy. Our firetruck is fitted with one large car that holds up to 18 people and is accessible to adults and children alike, including special needs persons.

Meet the Green Machine!

This train is comprised of up to 10 barrel cars and can hold up to 20 small children in total.This train does not accomodate adults and is safe and secure for small children with no risk for falling out when you follow our Rules & Regulations. It is perfect for festivals and birthday parties with several small children. It is fun to ride and watch as the train snakes its route and skillfully negotiates turns and donuts.
